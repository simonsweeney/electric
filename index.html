CLUB

3 dance spaces each with it's own sound and vibe and dancing room for up to 1200 people.

We are proud to support a family of local club nights, Irish and international underground DJ talent and electronic music legends from the worlds of house to hip-hop, techno, disco and grime.

Previous guests include: Hunee, Palms Trax, Omar S, Antal, Tama Sumo, Steffi & Virginia, Prosumer, Skream, Bicep, Dusky, Alan Fitzpatrick, Camelphat, DMX Krew, Ron Morelli.

BAR

we’ve got a MAIN BAR, a ROOFTOP BAR, a COCKTAIL BAR and we’ve even managed to squeeze in a RUM SHACK. Each room has it’s own distinct style and menu, from LOCAL and INTERNATIONAL craft BEERS to INVENTIVE COCKTAILS and spiked ‘LUSHIES’. We like to mix it up with LOCAL and EXOTIC INGREDIENTS such as rhubarb poitín and lychee & lemongrass whiskey.

MAIN BAR from 4PM.
ROOFTOP BAR from 6pm.

DINER

Biteclub - From a summer rooftop project(2014) to a full restaurant(2016) and serving bar bites throughout the venue, we can now proudly call Biteclub ‘Best Streetfood in Ireland’(2017).

Open from 4pm serving a menu of classics (Fried Chicken Burger, Vietnamese Bao’s, Fish Tacos); and seasonal bites (Popcorn Cauliflower, Duck Nuggets, Szechuan Shiitake dumplings).

We love a party and we regularly host Disco Brunches with food, drinks and DJs all day.

After dinner the restaurant becomes yet another part of the club for those who want to chill and enjoy a cocktail away from the madness of the dance floors.

Daily from 4pm.
Weekend Brunch from 12.

ROOFTOP

Eat, drink and dance al fresco.

Open from 6pm with 2 bars, a dance floor, a retractable roof and space for up to 400 people the rooftop is everyone’s favourite space.

Come nighttime the sun goes down and the volume goes up, turning the rooftop into an indoor-outdoor drinking, smoking and dancing space; and opening your night up to the full house below.

Wed-Sun from 6pm.
